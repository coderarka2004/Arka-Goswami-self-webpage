About Me

Hello, my name is Arka Goswami, and I am currently pursuing a Bachelor of Technology (BTech) degree in Electrical Engineering. Throughout my academic journey, I have developed a robust foundation in both theoretical and practical aspects of electrical engineering, including circuit analysis, power systems, control systems, and electronics.

In addition to my core electrical engineering studies, I have cultivated strong proficiency in coding. I am well-versed in several programming languages, including Python, C, and Matlab. Furthermore, I have hands-on experience with SQL and HTML, which has enabled me to work on diverse projects that integrate software and hardware solutions.

I am also proficient in TinkerCAD and Arduino, which I have used extensively for prototyping and developing various electronic projects. My interest in technology extends to the field of 3D printing, where I enjoy exploring innovative applications and solutions. This hobby complements my engineering skills and allows me to engage in creative problem-solving and prototyping.

With my comprehensive skill set, I am passionate about leveraging my technical expertise to contribute to advancements in technology and engineering. I am enthusiastic about the opportunities to collaborate with professionals in the field and apply my knowledge to real-world applications.

Thank you for taking the time to learn about me.
